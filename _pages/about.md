I am a professor at the **Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG)**, affiliated with the **Center for Computational Sciences (C3)**.  
My work spans **multi-agent systems**, **organizational knowledge**, **LLM-augmented distributed intelligence**, and **Digital Twin** architectures for **adaptive production systems**.

This website is designed as a **research hub** (narrative + program structure), complemented by my profiles on **Lattes**, **Google Scholar**, **ORCID**, and other platforms.
