# Eder Mateus Gonçalves

**Director, Center for Computational Sciences (C3) — Federal University of Rio Grande (FURG)**  
Researcher in **Organizational Multi-Agent Systems** and **AI-driven Production Systems**, bridging **collective intelligence**, **LLM-augmented distributed architectures**, and **Digital Twins** for adaptive cyber-physical environments.

## Research at a glance

My research program connects **foundational models of organizational coordination** to **deployable AI systems** in Industry 4.0/5.0 and beyond—supporting international collaboration and funding-oriented research with measurable impact.

- **Collective Intelligence & Organizational AI** (theoretical foundations)
- **LLM-Augmented Distributed Architectures** (cognitive layer)
- **AI-Driven Digital Twins** (cyber-physical layer)
- **Translational AI Systems** (industry & health-tech)
